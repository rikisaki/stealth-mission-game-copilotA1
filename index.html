  • 🔔 Reinforcement System - After 30s, other enemies go to last seen location (Sniper Elite mechanic)
  • 💥 Back Shots - Shoot from behind for 2x damage & bonus points!
  • 🔫 Wall Collision - Bullets blocked by walls
  • 🌱 Procedural Maps - Different layout every game (seed-based)
